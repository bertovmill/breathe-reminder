How it works

1

Download & open

Unzip and drop Breathe into your Applications folder. If macOS says it's "damaged", open Terminal and run: xattr -cr ~/Downloads/Breathe.app

2

Set your reminder

Use the built-in cron schedule or set your own reminder for 9pm each night.

3

Breathe

Follow the circle. 10 breaths. Mark it done. Close. That's it.

Built with care. No tracking. No accounts. Just breathing.
